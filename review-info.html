Smallest upcoming phone from Apple to be called iPhone 12 ‘mini’

Infinix Hot 10 price in India is expected to get cheaper with a brand new 4GB + 64GB variant that will launch soon, according to a new teaser image. By Sai Krishna - October 23. 2020

By Sai Krishna - October 23. 2020

HIGHLIGHTS
  • Infinix Hot 10 will get a new RAM and storage variant soon
  • The company is teasing a 4GB 64GB variant of the phone
  • Infinix Hot 10 price In India for the 6GB
  • 128GB option Is Rs 9,999

Infinix Hot 10 will get a new variant in India soon, according to a teaser shared by the company on its community page XClub. Going by the promo image, it scorns like the phone may come in a new variant that offers 4GB RAM and 64GB internal storage. To recall, the Infinix Hot 10 was launched in India with 6GB RAM and 128GB storage at Rs 9,999. The upcoming new variant Is expected to be priced even lower. Unfortunately, not much else has been revealed about the phone at the moment, so we do not know when this new Infinx Hot 10 RAM variant will be launching.

Also read: Infinix Note 8 and Note 8i launched with quad cameras, MediaTek Helio G80 SoC, 5,000mAh battery, and more

Infinix had recently launched the Hot 10 smartphone In India. The handset sports a 6.78-inch HD. IPS LCD display, 72001,640 pixels resolution, and a punch-hole cutout at the top left comer. The phone is powered by the MediaTek Helio G70 SoC paired with ARM Mall-G52, 6GB RAM, and 128GB storage that is further expandable via a microSD card slot. It runs Android 10 with XOS 7 custom skin on top. Connectivity features include 4G VoLTE, dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5, GPS, and micro-USB port.

As for the cameras, the Infinix Hot 10 has a quad-camera setup at the back comprising of a 16MP primary camera with quad-LED flash, a 2MP macro lens, a 2MP depth sensor, and a low-light video camera. It has an 8MP shooter on the front for selfies and video chats and a fingerprint sensor for security. A massive 5,200mAh battery with 18W standard charging support. Infinix Hot 10 price in India is set at Rs 9,999 for the 6GB 128GB option and is available via Flipkart.
